Background Images

A picture says a thousand words and Constellation want to enhance the image experience by providing easy to use classes for all your image needs. Images to span the entire width, break up sections and provide a bit of interest. Pop them on a div and watch your site take shape

Sample Background Images

Sample Code
<div class="container-xl">
<div class="row">
<div class="background-img position-center img-cover img-no-repeat"> </div>
</div>
</div>
Sample Code
<div class="container-xl">
<div class="row">
<div class="background-img position-left img-contain img-x-repeat"> </div>
</div>
</div>
Sample Code
<div class="container-xl">
<div class="row">
<div class="background-img position-top img-40 img-no-repeat"> </div>
</div>
</div>

Background Image Classes

All CSS properties available for background images are included in Constellation UI.

  • img-contain, img-cover, img-10 through to img-100 for background-size
  • repeat, repeat-x, repeat-y, no-repeat, space, round, initial, inherit for background repeat
  • top, left, center, bottom, right, left for background position
  • top left, bottom left, top right, bottom right for background position
