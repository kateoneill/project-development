Buttons

All websites utilise buttons. Use Constellation UI's buttons throughout your website. Constellation UI's buttons are fully customisable so add a selection of classes to produce the desired output, look and feel.

Sample Buttons

Constellation UI wants developers to have ultimate customisability, that's why there's a variety of classes provided to customise border radius, padding on all sides, colour, outline-colour and width.

Just add the following classes to a button class for customisation

  • Border Radius: border-radius-{value}
    • Choose any value between 1 and 100
  • Width: width-{value}
    • Choose a multiple of ten between 1 and 100
  • Padding: padding-{value}
    • Choose a value between 1 and 100
  • Padding-Left: padding-left-{value}
    • Choose a value between 1 and 100
  • Padding-Right: padding-right-{value}
    • Choose a value between 1 and 100
  • Padding-Top: padding-top-{value}
    • Choose a value between 1 and 100
  • Padding-Bottom: padding-bottom-{value}
    • Choose a value between 1 and 100
  • Margin: margin-{value}
    • Choose a value between 1 and 100
  • Margin-Left: margin-left-{value}
    • Choose a value between 1 and 100
  • Margin-Right: margin-right-{value}
    • Choose a value between 1 and 100
  • Margin-Top: margin-top-{value}
    • Choose a value between 1 and 100
  • Margin-Bottom: margin-bottom-{value}
    • Choose a value between 1 and 100
