Constellation UI offers horizontal and vertical card options as demonstrated above with ample customisability. Card width can vary from 1 to 100 using horizontal-{value} or vertical-{value} depending on orientation. Padding and margin are also fully editable and the cards can be integrated with other elements such as buttons or typography.
