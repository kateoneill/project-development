Typography

Good typography makes a good website great. Constellation UI supplies an option of Lato, Roboto, Roboto Slab and Merriweather for users to integrate with their sites.

Sample Typography Styling

Add any of these classes to your choice of heading tags, p tags, a tags or otherwise to include the relevant fonts. Colour varaitoins include white, black and a variety of greys numbered 1 through 11

h2 Roboto grey2

h2 RobotoSlab black

h2 Lato white

h2 Merriweather grey9

Sample Code
<h2 class="Roboto grey2">Roboto grey2 </h2>
<h2 class="RobotoSlab black">RobotoSlab black </h2>
<h2 class="Lato white">Lato white </h2>
<h2 class="Merriweather grey9">Merriweather grey9 </h2>

There's more to text than just the font choice, there's weights and styles to consider too

Sample Typography Styling

h2 Roboto light italic grey2

h2 RobotoSlab boldest black

h2 Lato semibold italic white

h2 Merriweather thin grey9

Sample Code
<h2 class="Roboto light italic grey2">Roboto light italic grey2 </h2>
<h2 class="RobotoSlab boldest black">RobotoSlab boldest black </h2>
<h2 class="Lato semibold italic white">Lato semibold italic white </h2>
<h2 class="Merriweather thin grey9">Merriweather thin grey9 </h2>

Other options for weight include : bold, regular medium, extra-light and extra-bold

On top of these font specific styling there's CSS attributes a developer should consider to bring some personality

Sample Typography Styling

h2 Roboto ls-3 text-uppercase light grey2

h2 RobotoSlab ti-25 regular black

h2 Lato semibold ws-15 italic white

h2 Merriweather lh-7 thin grey4 align-center

h2 RobotoSlab extra-light line-decoration-underline grey2

Sample Code
<h2 class="Roboto light ls-3 text-uppercase grey2">Roboto light ls-3 text-uppercase grey2 </h2>
<h2 class="RobotoSlab ti-25 regular black">RobotoSlab ti-25 regular black </h2>
<h2 class="Lato semibold ws-15 italic white">Lato semibold ws-15 italic white </h2>
<h2 class="Merriweather thin lh-7 grey4 text-align-center">Merriweather thin lh-7 grey4 align-center </h2>
<h2 class="RobotoSlab extra-light line-decoration-underline grey2">RobotoSlab extra-light line-decoration-underline grey2 </h2>

Other options for weight include : bold, regular medium, extra-light and extra-bold
