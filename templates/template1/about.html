About Me

Hi! I'm Dan or Daniel or Danny, depending on who I'm talking to. I'm from Vancouver, Canada and I love to travel.

I spent time working as a photographer for a travel agent so was always on the move and had so many photos to share. I decided to set up this blog to share my own adventures after leaving that job for more freedom. Though I miss the perks and my old co-workers and co-travellers, it's been refreshing meeting new people in whatever corner of the world I want as oppose to wherever my job sends me

I hope you'll join me on my adventure for travel tips. My favourite places, hidden gems and the likes while exploring cultural differences.

What I want?

I want to create a space for wanderlust travellers to interact with one another and get helpful tips. As a canadian I frequently have to travel for hours and for high cost to other parts of the world and other people will be saving for years for these trips so I want to help them uncover the best things to do on their travels. I want them to know the highlights of everywhere and avoid the duds. Hope I can help, hit me up on insta @travelwithdanmarch for more info :D

History

As I said earlier, I studied photography in college and got a job at a small magazine then graduated to a well-known global travel agent. I was sent on a mission across South East Asia to photograph along with 3 other photographers who ended up becoming some of my best friends. We travelled across Cambodia, Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia, Vietnam and Singapore. Saw the most amazing places and ate the most incredible food. When I got back I was so exhausted and decided to take a well-deserved break.

I pretty quickly yearned to travel again so undertook a trip across South America with my best friend, Maria and I wanted to share my photos so I set up this blog and it's taken me to so many amazing places and opened so many doors and afforded me amazing opportunities.
